About Bishal

AI engineer building trading models, automation, and films.

I build machine-learning systems for forex markets, automation pipelines that remove repetitive work, and AI-directed short films. My interest sits where rigorous engineering meets things people actually feel — a model that holds up out of sample, a workflow that runs without me, a scene that lands.

How I work

I start from the constraint, not the tool. A trading idea has to survive walk-forward testing before it earns a line of production code; an automation only ships once it handles its own failure cases. I prefer small systems that I understand completely over large ones I can only half-trust, and I write everything down so the next version starts from evidence instead of memory.

What I work on

  • Forex ML — feature pipelines, regime detection, and backtested signal models
  • Automation — Python and API workflows that replace manual, repeatable tasks
  • AI filmmaking — prompt-driven direction, shot continuity, and edit pipelines
  • Tooling — dashboards and internal apps that make the above legible

Approach

Measure first, automate second, polish last.

Most of my time goes into the unglamorous middle: cleaning data, defining what "working" means, and proving a system does it before scaling it up. The trading work taught me to respect variance and distrust a single good run. The filmmaking work taught me that taste is a skill you build through reps. I try to bring both habits to everything here.
